Asthma

Ayurvedic treatment for asthma focuses on improving lung function, reducing inflammation, and strengthening the respiratory system. It often includes herbal remedies, breathing exercises, and lifestyle modifications to manage symptoms and prevent asthma attacks.

Some commonly used medicinal herbs for asthma include:

  • Vasaka (Adhatoda)
  • Licorice
  • Tulsi (Holy Basil)
  • Pippali (Long Pepper)
  • Adulsa (Malabar Nut)
