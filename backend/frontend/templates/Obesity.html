Obesity

Ayurvedic treatment for obesity focuses on promoting weight loss, improving metabolism, and enhancing overall health and well-being. It typically includes herbal remedies, dietary modifications, lifestyle changes, and physical activity to achieve and maintain a healthy weight.

Some commonly used medicinal herbs for obesity include:

  • Guggul
  • Triphala
  • Garcinia Cambogia
  • Shilajit
  • Vidanga
