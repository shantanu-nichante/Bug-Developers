Anxiety & Stress

Ayurvedic treatment for anxiety and stress focuses on calming the mind, promoting relaxation, and balancing the nervous system. It often includes herbal remedies, lifestyle modifications, and stress management techniques to reduce anxiety symptoms and improve overall well-being.

Some commonly used medicinal herbs for anxiety and stress include:

  • Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri)
  • Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)
  • Jatamansi (Nardostachys jatamansi)
  • Shankhpushpi (Convolvulus pluricaulis)
  • Tulsi (Holy Basil)
