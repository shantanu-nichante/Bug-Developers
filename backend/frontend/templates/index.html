About Us

Mission

To revolutionize Ayurvedic Pharmaceutics by developing innovative solutions for the accurate identification of medicinal plants and raw materials through advanced Image Processing and Machine Learning Algorithms. Our mission is to address the challenges of misidentification, adulteration, and substitution in the medicinal plant industry, ensuring the supply chain integrity and fostering trust in the curative potential of traditional medicine.

Vision

We envision a future where the identification of medicinal plants and raw materials is seamless and accurate, contributing to the preservation of India's rich floral diversity. Our vision is to empower stakeholders in the Ayurvedic pharmaceutical industry, from collectors and traders to wholesalers and distributors, with a cutting-edge software solution. By leveraging technology, we aim to enhance the sustainability of medicinal plant resources, eliminate malpractices, and promote the growth of the MedTech/BioTech/HealthTech sector in India. Our commitment lies in creating a positive impact on the healthcare industry, fostering a harmonious coexistence between traditional wisdom and modern technology.

Target Market

The target market for the software includes Ayurvedic pharmaceutical companies, wholesalers, distributors, MedTech/BioTech/HealthTech sector, herbal extract manufacturers, research institutions, government agencies, ethical harvesters, and educational institutes in India. The software addresses the challenge of misidentification, adulteration, and substitution of medicinal plants, ensuring authenticity and quality across the supply chain.
