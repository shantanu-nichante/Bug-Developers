Hypertension

Ayurvedic treatment for hypertension focuses on lowering high blood pressure, improving cardiovascular health, and reducing the risk of complications associated with hypertension. It typically includes herbal remedies, dietary changes, lifestyle modifications, and stress management techniques to manage hypertension effectively.

Some commonly used medicinal herbs for hypertension include:

  • Garlic
  • Arjuna
  • Hawthorn
  • Brahmi (Gotu Kola)
  • Shankhpushpi (Convolvulus pluricaulis)
